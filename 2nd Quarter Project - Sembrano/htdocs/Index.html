Who Tells Your Story

The term "hard" is too simplistically used to characterize people's stories. We all have a tale to share that we won't tell just anyone. Life is tough in many ways. Thus, mental health is introduced. Our mental health includes all aspects of our physical, psychological, emotional, and social well-being (What Is Mental Health? | MentalHealth.gov, n.d.). It helps us cope with stress, interpersonal harmony, and make healthy decisions - but there are also some drawbacks. Issues that add a little complexity to daily life.
