The Categories of Mental Illnesses

Generally speaking, there are several types of mental health illnesses. Among the most typical are:
  • Eating Disorders - Is characterized by severe, long-lasting abnormalities in eating habits and is frequently linked to harmful body preoccupations. These conditions may have detrimental effects on health.
  • Personality Disorders - Are characterized by persistently maladaptive thought and behavior patterns that can make it challenging to establish or sustain relationships and cope with daily stress in both personal and professional life.
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorders - It is a type of mental illness when a traumatic event from the past—whether observed or experienced—causes extreme discomfort and interferes with daily life.
