Types of Personality Disorders

  • Schizotypal Personality Disorder - A pattern of bizarre or outlandish behaviour and trouble relating to others.
  • Borderline Personality Disorder - Problems with relationships, an ongoing pattern of impulsive conduct, fluctuating moods, and an unstable self-image.
  • Obsessive-Compulsive Personality Disorder - A pattern of binge-eating and purging, including self-induced vomiting.
