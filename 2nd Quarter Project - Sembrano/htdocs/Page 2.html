The Effects of Mental Illnesses

Mental Issues can not only affects one's mentality but also one's emotional and physical health.

    Effects of Mental Illnesses on Physical Health:

  • Chronic Diseases - Numerous chronic diseases and depression have been connected. Diabetes, asthma, cancer, cardiovascular disease, and arthritis are a few of these diseases.
  • "Brain Fog" - Sometimes it's difficult to focus or recall things, and your brain seems foggy and unfocused.
  • Substance Abuse - Addictions to alcohol, smoking, and recreational substances are more likely to develop in people with mental health issues.
  • Sleeping Problems
  • Stomach Pain

    • Effects of Mental Illnesses on Emotional Health:

  • Changes in Mood
  • Erratic Thinking
  • Chronic Anxiety
  • Exaggerated Sense of Self-worth
  • Impulsive Actions
