Types of Post-Traumatic Stress Disorders

  • Complex PTSD - PTSD caused by recurrent, persistent trauma (like long-term sexual or physical abuse) as opposed to a single traumatic event.
  • Delayed Expression PTSD - More than six months after the trigger event, PTSD may appear.
  • Dissociative PTSD - When remembering the traumatic incident, there is a clear dissociation, or derealization and depersonalization.
