Heroes of Olympus

Heroes of Olympus is a book about Greek and Roman god and demigods. The novel depicts the conflict between the two different demigods and Gaia. Gaia arose for she heard the news that his son- Kronos, has been defeated by a mere demigod- Percy Jackson. With the help of his new friends; Jason, Piper, Hazel, Leo, Frank- they would try and save the world.
