Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood is a dark fantasy and science fiction anime written by Japanese writer Hiromu Arakawa.
  • This anime gave me so much questions that could not be answered. And when I found out the answers, I was in shocked. This anime is jaw dropping and probably will make you cry. I recommend it!
  • It made me feel a lot of emotions, it made me cry, laugh, and angry. I am quite grateful for the experience. Lastly, if I could I wish I can watch this masterpiece for the first time again.
